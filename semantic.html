→ Crispy Chicken Lollipops

Introduction:

Chicken lollipops are a popular Indo-Chinese appetizer made with chicken winglets that have been marinated and coated in a batter and then deep-fried until crispy.They are a delicious and easy-to-make snack or appetizer that is sure to be a hit with your guests.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound chicken winglets, tips removed
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon ginger garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the soy sauce, ginger garlic paste, red chili powder, black pepper powder, and salt. Add the chicken winglets and toss to coat. Marinate for at least 30 minutes, or up to overnight.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, cornstarch, and egg.
  3. Dip the chicken winglets in the batter, coating them evenly.
  4. Heat the oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat. Fry the chicken winglets in batches until golden brown and cooked through, about 5 minutes per batch.
  5. Drain the chicken winglets on paper towels and serve immediately with your favorite dipping sauce.

Introduction:

Tandoori roti is a traditional Indian flatbread that is cooked in a tandoor oven. It is made with whole wheat flour, yogurt, and a few other ingredients. The rotis are cooked over high heat, which gives them a crispy exterior and a soft, fluffy interior. Tandoori roti is a popular accompaniment to Indian curries and stews.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour (atta)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Water, as needed

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the flour, salt, baking powder, and baking soda.
  2. Add the yogurt and oil and mix until a dough forms.
  3. Add water, a little at a time, and knead until the dough is soft and smooth.
  4. Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  5. Divide the dough into 6 equal pieces.
  6. Roll out each piece of dough into a thin circle.
  7. Heat a tawa (griddle) over medium heat.
  8. Cook each roti for 2-3 minutes per side, or until golden brown.
  9. Serve hot with your favorite Indian curry.

Introduction:

Khurbani ka meetha is a popular Hyderabadi dessert made with dried apricots. It is a sweet and tangy dish that is perfect for any occasion.The apricots are cooked in a sugar syrup until they are soft and plump, and then they are garnished with almonds and pistachios.Khurbani ka meetha is a delicious and refreshing dessert that is sure to please everyone.

Ingredients:

  • 25-30 dried apricots, pitted
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 1/4 cup almonds, sliced
  • 1/4 cup pistachios, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the apricots and soak them in water for at least 2 hours, or overnight.
  2. Drain the apricots and add them to a pot with the sugar and water.
  3. Bring the mixture to a boil over medium heat, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the apricots are soft and the syrup has thickened.
  4. Stir in the cardamom powder and remove the pot from the heat.
  5. Let the apricots cool in the syrup, then garnish with the almonds and pistachios..
  6. Serve chilled or at room temperature.
